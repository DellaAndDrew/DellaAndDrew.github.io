Hotels and Transit


There are two locations for wedding events. The rehearsal dinner is between Chapel Hill and Durham (the red pin), and the ceremony and reception are at Fearrington Village (the yellow pin), a 20-minute drive from Chapel Hill and a 35-minute drive from RDU airport. We recommend three hotels. Two are in Chapel Hill, which is between the rehearsal dinner and ceremony, and one is at Fearrington, the location for the ceremony and reception.

The Fearrington House Inn (Yellow Pin)

From $350 per night, at the site of the ceremony and reception. The hotel is small, so if you are interested in staying here we suggest booking early. Fearrington is farther from the rehearsal dinner and airport than Chapel Hill.

The Carolina Inn (Green Pin)

From $200 per night, on Franklin Street in downtown Chapel Hill. Parking is $18 per day.

The Hampton Inn and Suites, Carrboro (Green Pin)

From $150 per night, in downtown Carrboro (right next to downtown Chapel Hill). Parking is $12 per day.

Should we rent a car or take Uber?

Unless you want to visit Durham on Saturday, ride sharing is probably cheaper. The risk is that there could be a wait to get a ride from Fearrington on Saturday night. Renting a car is definitely more convenient, but Chapel Hill hotels charge for parking. Either way, we recommend carpooling!

What should I do on Saturday?


Duke Gardens

A huge English-style garden next to the Duke campus. Even if the flowers haven't bloomed, the arboretum, duck pond, and Japanese garden make for a nice walk. Admission is free but parking is not, unless you park elsewhere and walk.

Duke Campus

Objectively prettier than UNC! Be sure to visit the gardens, which are right next to campus.

Carrboro Farmer's Market

From 7 to noon at 301 West Main Street in Carrboro.

The Spa at Fearrington

If you have any interest, this needs no explanation.

Wine Tasting at Fearrington

Free alcohol! From 2-5pm in the Belted Goat at Fearrington Village.

Downtown Durham

Downtown is fun and worth a visit. Our favorite spots are:

  • Brightleaf Square: shops to browse in a cute square
  • American Tobacco District: a pleasant, recently renovated area where the tobacco factories used to be. Right next to the minor league ballpark and the irresistible Mellow Mushroom.
  • Downtown bars and restaurants: we're fond of Thai@Main, Pour Taproom, the Parlour, and, of course, the Mellow Mushroom. Yelp is sure to have other recommendations.

Rise Biscuits and Donuts

Truly the best dang biscuits and donuts. Locations in Carrboro (walkable from downtown Chapel Hill), downtown Durham, and Southpoint (between Durham and Chapel Hill).

FAQ


What's the dress code?
Please wear church-appropriate clothes to the ceremony and reception. There is no dress code for the rehearsal dinner.

Should we bring gifts?
No, please do not bring any gifts.

Okay, I recognize that you do not want gifts. But what if I absolutely insist on bringing a gift anyway?
Fine. If you really, really want to get us a gift (which you shouldn't), contact us for a few ideas.

Should we take Uber or rent a car?
Uber is probably cheaper (unless you want to move a lot on Saturday), but it might be hard to get a ride from the reception. Renting a car and carpooling might be easiest.
